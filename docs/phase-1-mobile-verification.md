# Phase 1 Mobile Verification Checklist

## Device Checks

1. Open the creator on a phone-sized viewport.
2. Confirm the sticky top status card and bottom action bar stay clear of safe-area insets.
3. Swipe horizontally across the active category body to move between categories.
4. Scroll vertically inside a category and confirm the page does not accidentally switch categories.
5. Tap category chips as a fallback and confirm they mirror swipe navigation.
6. Switch between Female and Futa-Female; verify the futa-only appendage field appears only in the supported mode.
7. Open a sheet-select field, choose an option, and close the sheet with the close button.
8. Switch reduced motion on in the OS and confirm category transitions feel instant.

## Expected Outcomes

- Swipe changes category only after a clear horizontal gesture.
- Vertical scroll remains reliable while reading field cards.
- No unsupported identities or free-text inputs appear.
- Rule notices explain disabled or missing selections inline.
